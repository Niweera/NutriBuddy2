How to count calories per day?


As you now know, Calories are the unit to measure intake energy or food energy and already stored energy in our body. Food energy is what we obtain from eating foods. This is the energy we mostly measure as calories per day or counting calories. All foods have different amount of energy or calories in them. Some have a lot of calories and some have a lot less.
